# one-bowl Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

## What we collect

- **Email address** — for account creation and sign-in only
- **Taste records** — your ratings, notes, and photos of food and drinks you choose to submit
- **Location data** — only when you use the "Near me" feature, and only while the app is open
- **Photos** — only photos you explicitly choose to upload with a taste record

## What we don't do

- We don't sell your data to anyone
- We don't show ads
- We don't track you across other apps
- We don't share your email with third parties

## How we use your data

Your taste records, ratings, and photos are visible to other app users. This is the core purpose of the app — sharing taste-based food ratings. Your email address is never shown publicly.

## Data storage

Your data is stored securely on Supabase (cloud infrastructure hosted on AWS). Photos are stored in Supabase Storage.

## Deleting your data

You can delete your own taste records from within the app. To delete your entire account and all associated data, email us at hello@tastereference.com and we will process it within 30 days.

## Third-party services

- **Google Places API** — to search for restaurants and locations
- **Supabase** — database and authentication
- **Foodpanda, GrabFood, Deliveroo** — affiliate ordering links (opens in external browser, subject to their own privacy policies)

## Changes

We may update this policy. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
